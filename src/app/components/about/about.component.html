About Us

JustFoodie is an elegant fine dining restaurant with an emphasis on natural, sustainable food, sourced from the Nigeria's most dedicated farmers and food producers. We create beautifully crafted dishes, seeking out the best of African produce, with a passion to delight and share our curiosity and our love of delicious artisanal food.

We offer a 10 course tasting menu that changes with the seasons and is produce and flavour driven.

… and most importantly - TASTED!
